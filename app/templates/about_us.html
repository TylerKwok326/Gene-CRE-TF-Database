Our Mission

At GeneInteract, our mission is to create an integrative database focused on the regulatory interplay between transcription factors (TFs), genes, and cis-regulatory elements (CREs) in microglia. By uncovering the complex mechanisms behind brain and eye disorders, such as Alzheimerâ€™s, glaucoma, and multiple sclerosis, we aim to bridge the gap between genetic risk variants and dysregulated gene expression. Our goal is to empower researchers with the resources and tools to explore these regulatory connections, providing critical insights that accelerate therapeutic discovery for neurodegenerative diseases and neuroinflammation. Through our platform, we seek to facilitate the identification of novel targets and regulatory circuits, ultimately contributing to advancements in precision medicine.
